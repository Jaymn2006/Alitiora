This report presents the research, conceptual development, strategic planning, architectural design, and innovation analysis that led to the creation of ALITIORA.

2. PROBLEM IDENTIFICATION RESEARCH
A. Creator Platform Fragmentation
Research found creators must use multiple unrelated platforms to complete workflows.
Challenges include:
•	Context switching
•	Subscription overload
•	Tool incompatibility
•	High learning curves
________________________________________
B. Financial Exclusion
Major payment systems often exclude regions dependent on:
•	Mobile money
•	Localized digital wallets
•	Alternative payment rails
Particularly underserved:
•	African creators
•	Emerging-market innovators
•	Youth creators without banking access
________________________________________
C. Limited AI Accessibility
Existing AI builder platforms often:
•	Require coding knowledge
•	Lack autonomous response systems
•	Do not support creator-specific workflows
________________________________________
D. Weak Intellectual Property Protection
Independent creators often face:
•	Content theft
•	Unauthorized reposting
•	Limited legal recourse
________________________________________
E. Lack of Creator Investment Access
Most creators cannot easily secure micro-investment support for early-stage projects.
